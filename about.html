ABOUT US

ANAND FILTERS located at Bangalore is a privately owned company manufacturing and distributing all types of Air-filters for air intake application for HVAC systems, air handling units, Clean room requirements etc. The Company’s aim is to provide high quality Air Filters to meet the growing demand at affordable prices. Besides this, the company manufactures filters of allied designs too.

The promoters of this company are qualified engineers. They have vast experience in the filter industry having worked for more than two decades in it in varied capacities including manufacturing, quality, and testing. They are endowed with thorough knowledge of industrial and automotive filters. The company was established in 2004.

We have adequate infrastructure for in-house fabrication, assembly of filters and filter media testing. Our qualified staff is ready to offer suggestions and provide support to customers on technical matters as and when required. Our products are marketed by our own sales force and through competent distributors. The product range, which focuses on innovative AHU filters, is constantly expanding and our ambition is to develop and reach new parameters and harness new technologies in keeping with the ever-changing needs of our valued customers.
